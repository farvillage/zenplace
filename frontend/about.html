About ZenPlace

ZenPlace is a virtual wellness environment built to offer a safe space for listening and reflection. Using artificial intelligence, it lets you vent your thoughts and feelings without judgment, while also learning about practices like Zazen Meditation, Yoga, Lo-Fi music, and ASMR.

ZenPlace is a secondary emotional support tool and does not replace qualified mental health professionals (psychologists, psychiatrists, and others). In a crisis, call the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text 988, free, 24/7).
